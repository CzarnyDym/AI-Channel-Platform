# AI Channel Consultant

# North Star

The product should answer one question better than anyone else:

**"Why is my channel growing or declining, and what should I do next?"**

## Core Idea

- [ ] Become an AI consultant for creators, not just a comment analyzer.
- [ ] Help creators understand WHY videos succeed or fail.
- [ ] Turn comments into actionable business insights.
- [ ] Support multiple social media platforms.
